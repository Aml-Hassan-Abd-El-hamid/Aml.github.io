WHAT IS THIS BLOG FOR?

  • by:Tanja Adžić
  • date:15-12-2021

Hi, welcome to my blog. This introductory post serves as a guide to my Blog and Project posts. The guide will help you find what you are looking for in a quick way, regarding the subject you are interested in. I will be posting once every two weeks.

Blog Structure

The Blog is divided into three different categories:

  • HOW TO
  • SELF-LEARNING
  • PROJECT

The HOW TO category is meant for explaining a technique or a concept in Data Science to a non-technical audience. For example: you may find various topics here covering data cleaning, feature engineering, training, machine learning and deep learning in general, including statistics and programming in Python. The goal of these posts is to explain a concept in the simplest way possible, and present it in an efficient way.

The SELF-LEARNING category focuses on concepts and techniques I am currently learning, and the goal is to be able to explain these concepts in a simple manner in order to show the level of understanding it.

The PROJECT category is reserved for completed projects. It will usually focus on presenting all components of a projects start-to-finish, as well as the flow of thinking that led to various decisions in the project.

Projects Structure

The Project pages is another component of my portfolio, and it includes case studies, and completed projects showcases. Depending on the project and its goals, it can be presented in different formats. Project posts will be categorized by the technique applied to a specific case studie i.e. Natural Language Processing, Recommender System. These case studies will also be used in blog posts as an example.

Enjoy my blog, and do not hesitate to contact me regarding any questions you might have.
